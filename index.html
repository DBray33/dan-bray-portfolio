About Me

I’m Dan Bray, a motivated and detail-oriented professional with a passion for creativity and technology. After 8 years of excelling in the craft beer industry, I’m transitioning my skills and experience into a career in web development and digital design. Throughout my career, I’ve honed my abilities in project management, web and graphic design, social media strategy, and e-commerce development. At Broken Chair Brewery, I managed every facet of operations, from crafting award-winning beer recipes to spearheading digital marketing campaigns. My efforts elevated the brewery to become a community favorite and the county’s “Best Craft Brewery” every year the award was available. Beyond brewing, I designed all visual branding, including can labels, apparel, and merchandise, while also managing the brewery’s website and social media presence. In addition to my brewery career, I’ve built successful e-commerce stores and provided freelance graphic design services. These ventures allowed me to design and market custom merchandise across platforms like Shopify, Etsy, and eBay, where my designs achieved top search rankings. I’m currently advancing my web development skills through certifications and hands-on projects. My portfolio reflects my dedication to creating impactful digital solutions, leveraging tools like the MERN stack, React, and WordPress.
