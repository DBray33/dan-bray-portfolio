About Me

I’m Dan Bray, a motivated and detail-oriented professional with a passion for creativity and technology. After 8 years of excelling in the craft beer industry, I’m transitioning my skills and experience into a career in web development and digital design. Throughout my career, I’ve honed my abilities in project management, web and graphic design, social media strategy, and e-commerce development. At Broken Chair Brewery, I managed every facet of operations, from crafting award-winning beer recipes to spearheading digital marketing campaigns. My efforts elevated the brewery to become a community favorite and the county’s “Best Craft Brewery” every year the award was available (they got rid of it).

Beyond brewing, I designed all visual branding, including can labels, apparel, and merchandise, while also managing the brewery’s website and social media presence. In addition to my brewery career, I’ve built successful e-commerce stores and provided freelance graphic design services. These ventures allowed me to design and market custom merchandise across platforms like Shopify, Etsy, and eBay, where my designs achieved top search rankings. I’m currently advancing my web development skills through certifications and hands-on projects. My portfolio showcases my commitment to crafting impactful digital solutions while enhancing my proficiency in tools such as the MERN stack and WordPress, and reflects my passion for building custom-coded websites.

Development Approach

In every project, my approach to web development is rooted in precision, creativity, and a deep commitment to delivering impactful results. Transitioning from a career managing the complexities of the craft beer industry to web development, I have honed a meticulous attention to detail, a problem-solving mindset, and an unwavering focus on user satisfaction. I am obsessed with creating movements and functionalities that make sense, feel intuitive, and are inherently satisfying—whether it's scroll behavior, hover characteristics, or ensuring elements adapt seamlessly and beautifully as the viewport changes. Here’s how I bring concepts to life:

1. Discovery and Planning

  • Begin by understanding the client's vision, goals, and target audience.
  • Conduct comprehensive research to define project scope, timelines, and deliverables.
  • Collaborate to ensure alignment on aesthetics, functionality, and user needs.

2. Design with Purpose

  • Focus on clean, user-centered layouts that enhance usability and accessibility, with movements and interactions that feel natural and engaging.
  • Optimize hover effects, animations, and scroll behaviors to create a seamless and satisfying user experience.
  • Ensure all designs are mobile-first, fully responsive, and intuitive.

3. Development with Precision

  • Optimize hover effects, animations, and scroll behaviors to create a seamless and satisfying user experience.

4. E-Commerce and SEO Optimization

  • Expertise in launching and managing e-commerce platforms, including Shopify and Squarespace.
  • Integrate and manage features such as print-on-demand apparel stores to expand functionality and increase revenue.
  • Optimize all platforms for search engines, ensuring high visibility and user engagement.

5. Deployment and Hosting
